What Is Structural Remodeling?

Structural remodeling means modifying the elements that support your home—things like load-bearing walls, beams, joists, and foundation systems. It’s what allows you to go from a cramped, closed-off kitchen to an open-concept living space. Or take an unfinished basement and turn it into a legal bedroom or family room with proper support and egress.

In Federal Way, many homes were built decades ago with layouts that don’t fit how we live today. Structural remodeling gives you the freedom to modernize without moving.
